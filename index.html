About

I am a university student and entrepreneur interested in exploring the intersection of product creation and software. Over the course of 2020 I went way out of my comfort zone to try new things and started up my small business, LittleBigCreations, to sell handmade watercolour cards and custom art pieces. I also co-founded 2 NPOs, Grapevine Networking (helping students to explore their passions) and Project Penn (providing women with the required resources and mentorship to proceed in their professional lives). I strongly believe that it is equally important to give back to the community and help our future generations, and so I strive to do my best to help out the community through these projects.

Travelling, exploring and trying new things has always been a life motto that I lived by and this helps me with my creative vision on a daily. My visionary mindset has opened many doors for me to join various clubs and projects and to meet a diverse group of people who push me to be the best version of myself and grow as an individual on the daily. I continue to use my inspirations from such journeys to tackle challenges, develop app prototypes and innovate solutions that I pitch at hackathons and competitions in hopes of sharing my visions and making society's lives more efficient. I’m excited to see what the 2021 year has in store for me!
